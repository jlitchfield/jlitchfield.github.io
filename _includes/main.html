Integrate Design System

The Integrate app was a fragmented user experience and badly needed to fresh start. A principled design system with leadership continuity would mean that customers would have a consistent and coherent user experience.

Also, design principles drives better communication and decision making. Planning along with streamlined design and development workflows boosts overall productivity that results in delivering functionality to end customers faster, giving Integrate an edge over its competitors.
